Sports Medicine

My interests are in the intersection of technology and human health

Specific Areas of Interest (as of now)

  • Biomechanics - Understanding how the body moves and functions during athletic performance

    • I really like the work that P3 does with data analytics, biomechanics, and athletic performance.

  • Sports Technology - Exploring the use of technology to enhance athletic performance and training
  • Injury Prevention - Developing strategies and technologies to prevent sports-related injuries
  • Tendon Health - Research and applications focused on tendon function, recovery, and optimization

    • I have a personal interest in understanding and improving tendon health, especially given my own experiences with injuries. The UC Davis Muscle and Tendon labs are doing some really cool stuff

  • Sports Rehabilitation - Engineering new devices and technologies to aid recovery from sports injuries in athletes

    • I am working on building some concussion baseline testing and rehab devices

Why Sports Medicine?

I am a current college basketball player with a extensive history of injuries, specifically tendon related.
